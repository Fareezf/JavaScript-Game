How to Play

1. Use the WASD or arrow keys to move.
2. In the light dimension, navigate through the course, avoid the obstacles and complete 3 levels.
3. Once 3 levels are complete, shift reality from light to dark.
4. Press the F or J key to attack. 5. In the dark dimension, defeat all the enemies and complete the level.
6. There are 5 stages in total. Every normal stage has 4 levels, while the boss levels only have one. The first 3 levels is to collect stars and the last level is to fight a wave of enemies
7. There is also a boss fight at the end level.
8. You have 2 minutes to complete each BOSS level.
Good luck!
