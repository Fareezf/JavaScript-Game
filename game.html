How to Play

  • Use the WASD or arrow keys to move
  • In the light dimension, navigate through the course, avoid the obstacles, and complete 3 levels.
  • Once 3 levels are complete, reality will shift from light to dark
  • In the dark dimension, defeat all the enemies and complete the level
  • There are 5 levels in total. There are also random boss fights.
  • There is also a boss fight at the end level
  • You have 2 minutes to complete each BOSS level
Good luck!
